Who am I??

Hi, I'm Alexandra but popularly known as Sasha Sloan. This is my website and on it I talk about myself, show me some music. If you like my songs, sign up and have the freedom to listen to them :)

MY MUSIC IS MY LIFE

Older (musical premiere) - Sasha

The song portrays the maturing of the relationship with my parents, many times I didn't like being in my house and I didn't like my parents and I felt that I didn't fit into that family, I tried to escape that reality that existed. Over time I realized that relationships are actually difficult, we are all human and we make mistakes, this made me realize that sometimes it's better to let someone go than to get hurt.

Lie (acoustic music) - Sasha

I wrote lie after I had left a relationship that was dead for a long time. It was the first relationship either one of us had been in and I think we knew we weren’t right for each other but didn’t know how to fix it. I kept trying to make it work as hard as I could because I was so scared of it ending. I wanted it to work so desperately I was lying to the other person as well as myself. As our relationship started to deteriorate I found companionship in strangers on the internet.

House With No Mirrors - Sasha

For as long as I can remember I’ve struggled with body image. I developed an eating disorder as a teenager and I’ve been living with it ever since. I have always hated the way I looked and it’s gotten in the way of me living life. I’ve missed out on a lot of things that should’ve been fun because I was too self-conscious. If I see a bad photo of me or a video of me that I don’t like it won’t just ruin my day but it will become actually debilitating.
